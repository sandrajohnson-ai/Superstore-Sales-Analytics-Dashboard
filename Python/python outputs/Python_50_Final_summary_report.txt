Final Summary Report
====================
Total records analyzed: 51,290.
Total columns in the dataset: 21.
The dataset contains no missing values.
The Sales column was cleaned and converted to numeric format for analysis.
The Technology and Furniture categories generated high sales.
APAC and US markets contributed significantly to total sales.
Monthly sales analysis showed that sales vary across different months, with some months performing better than others.
The pivot table provided a summary of sales by category and market.
Correlation analysis showed a strong positive relationship (0.768) between Sales and Shipping Cost.
There is a negative relationship (-0.316) between Discount and Profit, indicating that higher discounts generally reduce profit.
This analysis demonstrates the use of Pandas for data cleaning, filtering, grouping, aggregation, pivot tables, and business insights.
